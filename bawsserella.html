Unity Web Player | BAWSserella

"Boss Up"-themed game made for the ~16-hour GAMERella 2014 game jam in Montreal.
Team: Anh Chi Bui, Ashwath George, Andrew Goudreau, Sora Park, and Elena Vegas.

Space to start the game (or restart from the win screen).
Level 1

  • LMB or Z to throw a projectile to the cursor.
Level 2
  • RMB or X to swipe at nearby enemies nearby enemies on either side of you.
  • LMB or Z to throw a projectile to the cursor.
Level 3
  • MMB or C to shoot a laser beam.
  • RMB or X to swipe at nearby enemies nearby enemies on either side of you.
  • LMB or Z to throw a projectile to the cursor.
